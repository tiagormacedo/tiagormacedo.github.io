Tiago Macedo

I am an Associate Professor of Mathematics at the Federal University of São Paulo (Unifesp), in Brazil. In the past, I have been a postdoctoral fellow at the University of Ottawa (in Canada) and an Assistant Professor at the Federal University of Catalão (in Brazil).

I am currently working on a research project and teaching teaching two undergraduate classes at Unifesp. I am also an editor of the journal Communications in Mathematics. More detailed information about me can be found in my Currículo Lattes and on my GitHub profile.

The best way to contact me is via email at tmacedo@unifesp.br.

Research

I am interested in homological algebra, representation theory, combinatorial optimization, and operations research.
